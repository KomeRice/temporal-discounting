TUTO 3 : Unlock the Second Method
We will now learn how to unlock the second method. Initially, the second 
method associated with each symbol is not available. It is locked by several lockers (2 lockers 
in this tutorial). 

At each trial you can unlock only one locker by 

1- clicking on the button “unlock” and then

2- moving the handle of the slider back and forth until the locker is white and opened.
            
Follow these steps to unlock the second method :
                
1. Select all the forms corresponding to the target form.
            
2. Here, instead of clicking on the next button, click on the unlock button 
underneath the target symbol.
3. The slider that just appear need to be 'slided' to unlock one padlock.
So slide the slider back and forth (from the right to the left then from the left to the right) until it 
disappear and a padlock turn white.
                
4. Now click on the next button and reiterate that process one more time.
                
5. Use the second mode for this form.
