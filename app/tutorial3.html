Tutorial: Unlocking Expert Mode
During the experiment, Expert Mode is not unlocked by default.
Expert Mode may be unlocked for each shape by removing each of its corresponding locks.
Unlock progress is not shared between shapes.

On every step, you may remove a lock for the ongoing target shape by committing time to perform a small task.

To unlock the Expert mode:
                
1. Click on every shape in the grid matching the target.
            
2. Instead of clicking "Next", click on the "Unlock" shown below the target.
A slider will then be taking its place.
3. Click and move the slider's handle back and forth until it disappears.
The task will be completed and a lock for the shape will be removed.
                
4. Repeat until all locks are removed.
                
5. Expert Mode may now be used to complete the step.
